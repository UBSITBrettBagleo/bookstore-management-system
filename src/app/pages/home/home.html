Welcome to Twilight Pages Bookstore

Welcome to the Bookstore Management System. This application allows you to manage your bookstore inventory by viewing, adding, editing, and deleting books stored in the MongoDB database.

📚 Books

View all books currently stored in the database. You can edit or delete existing books.

📊 Dashboard

View inventory statistics including total books, stock, inventory value, and genres.

➕ Add Book

Add a new book by entering its title, author, genre, price, stock, and cover image.
